Reflections

I am currently a third year Industrial Design student, studying at Victoria University of Wellington. I am a bubbly hardworking individual who loves problem solving and working with people. Creating great customer and user experience is what I strive for. I love to design and create products that look sleek, spark interest and have seamless user interaction. My choice to study industrial design stemmed from my love for learning creatively. I Love to see my work progress from the initial design phase to a physical prototype. My passions include spending time outdoors in particular on the water whether it be boating, paddle boarding or fishing. I also love to travel and listen to music. My passions are usually the inspiration behind my work.
